Crown-shyness

fromm wikipedia,the free encyclopedia For the album by Trash Boat,see Crown Shyness(album) Crown shyness(also canopy disengagement,[1] canopy shyness[2] or inter-crown spacing[3]) is a phenomenon observed in the same treespecies,in which the crowns of fully stocked trees do not touch each other, forming a canopy with channel-like gaps .[4][5]The phenomenon is most prevalent among trees of the same species.but also occurs between trees of different species.[6][7]There exist many hypothese as to why crown shyness is an adaptive behavior.and research suggests that it might inhabit spread of leaf-eatting insect larvae.[8]
